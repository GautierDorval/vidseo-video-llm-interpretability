# Use cases

VidSEO is useful when the meaning of a page depends heavily on what is said in a video.

## Common use cases

### Tutorials and walkthroughs

A long explanation is easier to scan when the transcript is available as text on the page.

### Interviews and talks

Important quotes become easier to locate, reuse, and reference.

### Product demos

Visitors can read the explanation even if they do not play the full video.

### Educational content

Transcript text helps preserve the structure of an explanation.

### Accessibility-oriented publishing

When audiovisual content needs a text alternative, transcript exposure helps.

## Typical goals

Publishers often use VidSEO when they want to:
- make spoken explanations available as text,
- reduce the opacity of video-only pages,
- reuse existing subtitles more effectively,
- keep control over transcript wording.

## Not the goal

VidSEO is not a promise engine.

It does not guarantee:
- improved rankings,
- answer-engine inclusion,
- quoted answers,
- product authority,
- transcript correctness by default.
