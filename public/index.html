VidSEO

Video transcript embedding for WordPress

Make video content readable as text — without turning the product site into a policy site.

VidSEO embeds videos and exposes their transcripts as native HTML on the same page. That gives search engines, screen readers, and answer engines a clear text surface to read.

View plugin on WordPress.org Read the docs
  • YouTube and Vimeo support
  • YouTube subtitles when available
  • Manual transcript support
  • Native HTML output

Why VidSEO exists

When useful information lives only inside video or audio, machines still have to approximate what the page is about.

VidSEO solves that very specific problem by exposing transcript text directly in the page, close to the embedded video.

Readable by machines

Transcript text becomes part of the page content, not something hidden behind video playback.

Readable by people

Visitors can scan, search, quote, and revisit what was said without replaying the entire video.

Editable by publishers

You keep editorial control over the transcript and its structure.

What VidSEO does

  • embeds YouTube or Vimeo videos
  • associates a video with a transcript
  • renders transcript content as HTML inside the page
  • keeps transcript text close to the video context
  • supports a shortcode-based publishing workflow

What VidSEO does not do

  • it does not generate content
  • it does not summarize or rewrite transcripts
  • it does not invent missing facts
  • it does not guarantee rankings, visibility, or citations
Important: this site is about VidSEO the product. The machine-readable files are still published, but they are supporting infrastructure, not the main story.
